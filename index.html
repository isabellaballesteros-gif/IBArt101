Shih Tzu

The Imperial Lion Dog

Regal Shih Tzu

Personality

Affectionate, playful, and outgoing. Shih Tzus are known for their friendly nature and love for human companionship.

Size

Small breed, typically 9-16 pounds and 8-11 inches tall. Perfect for apartment living and families of all sizes.

Grooming

Long, flowing coat requires daily brushing and regular professional grooming to maintain their beautiful appearance.

Exercise

Moderate exercise needs. Daily walks and indoor play are sufficient to keep these little lions happy and healthy.

About Shih Tzus

The Shih Tzu, meaning "lion dog" in Chinese, is an ancient breed that was treasured by Chinese royalty. These small but sturdy dogs are known for their distinctive long, flowing coats and sweet, gentle personalities. Originally bred as companion dogs for Chinese emperors, Shih Tzus have maintained their regal bearing while becoming beloved family pets worldwide.

Key Characteristics:

  • Excellent with children and other pets
  • Low to moderate exercise requirements
  • Adaptable to various living situations
  • Long lifespan (10-18 years)
  • Minimal shedding (with proper grooming)

Double Doodle

The Ultimate Designer Dog

Adorable Double Doodle

Personality

Intelligent, friendly, and highly trainable. Double Doodles inherit the best traits from both Poodles and Golden Retrievers.

Size

Medium to large breed, typically 50-80 pounds and 20-26 inches tall. Great for active families with space.

Grooming

Curly, low-shedding coat requires regular brushing and professional grooming every 6-8 weeks.

Exercise

High energy breed needs daily exercise including walks, playtime, and mental stimulation activities.

About Double Doodles

A Double Doodle is a cross between a Goldendoodle (Golden Retriever + Poodle) and a Labradoodle (Labrador Retriever + Poodle). This "designer dog" combines the intelligence and hypoallergenic qualities of Poodles with the friendly, outgoing nature of Golden Retrievers and Labradors. The result is an incredibly smart, loving, and versatile family companion.

Key Characteristics:

  • Hypoallergenic and low-shedding
  • Excellent with children and families
  • Highly intelligent and trainable
  • Great for active lifestyles
  • Versatile working and therapy dogs
